# Navigation

- Open Homepage -> Homepage is loaded
- Click "For agencies" in the header -> For Agencies page is opened, "Streamline licensing for your clients" is visible
- Click "For brand owners" in the header -> For Brand Owners page is opened, "Your brand deserves better deals. Start finding them here." is visible
- Click "Explore" button -> Dropdown menu opens
- Click "Explore" button - Click "Marketplace" -> Marketplace page is opened, brand cards with "View" buttons are visible
- Click "Explore" button - Click "Opportunities" -> Explore Opportunities page is opened, "Explore opportunities" text is visible
- Click "Explore" button - Click "Pricing" -> Pricing page is opened, "Frequently asked questions" and "Talk to sales" button are visible
- Click "Log in" button -> Login page is opened, login form is visible
- Open any page - Click Negosh logo -> Homepage is opened, "Sign up" and "Explore" buttons are visible
- Click "Sign up" button -> Sign Up page is opened, form fields are visible
