# Sign Up

- Open Homepage -> Homepage is loaded
- Open Homepage - Click "Sign up" button -> Sign Up page is opened, Email, Password, First Name, Last Name, Company Name, Business Website, Role selector are visible
- Open Homepage - Click "Sign up" button - Click "Sign up" button without filling any fields -> 6 validation messages "Please fill out this field." are displayed next to: Email, Password, First Name, Last Name, Company Name, and Terms of Use. No validation for Business Website (not required)
- Open Homepage - Click "Sign up" button - Click "Log in" link -> Login page is opened, Email input, Password input, and "Log in" button are visible
